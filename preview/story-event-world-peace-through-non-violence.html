Event | "World Peace through Non-Violence"

November 2018

← All Stories

The “World Peace through Non-Violence” function was organized by Acharya Dr. Lokesh Muni and his Ahimsa Vishwa Bharti organization on the 150th Birthday celebrations of Mahatma Gandhi at Bombay Stock Exchange Auditorium. In his address, Syedna TUS proposes action plans for peace and the roles religious leaders can play during tumultuous times of discord and violence. Click here to read further highlights about the event and view photos.
