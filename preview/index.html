Zahra Hasanaat

Service. Education.
Harmony.

Founded on the vision of Syedna Khuzaima Qutbuddin RA, Zahra Hasanaat uplifts communities through education, healthcare, financial inclusion, interfaith dialogue, and compassionate service.

Explore Our Programs Support Our Work

الخلق عيال الله، واحب الخلق الى الله انفعهم لعياله

“All of creation are the children of God, and the most beloved to God of those children is the one who benefits His children the most.”

— Rasulullah SA

Established in 1996, Zahra Hasanaat helps individuals and families realise their aspirations in health, education, and finance — guided by the belief that upliftment, not charity, benefits both the giver and the receiver and builds a society rooted in dignity, harmony, and compassion.

Our Programs

Serving communities through six pillars of impact.

Education

The Qutbi Jubilee Scholarship Program supports students from primary school through higher education with scholarships, mentorship, and career guidance.

Learn more →

Interfaith

Taqreeb fosters inter- and intra-faith understanding through academic conferences, grassroots community service, and dialogue.

Learn more →

Health

Medical camps, mental health awareness, dental care, eye care, insurance guidance, and wellness webinars for underserved communities.

Learn more →

Financial Advisory

Financial literacy, livelihood support, small business loans, and advisory services to help families achieve economic stability.

Learn more →

Food Security

Food distribution programs ensuring no family goes hungry, guided by the Qur’anic principle of feeding and clothing those in need.

Learn more →
