Art Wellness Workshop

Art Wellness Workshop
 Date: Thursday, May 29
Time: 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Venue: Zahra Hasanaat Center
Registration Fee: ₹100 (20 seats only) - includes lunch

What to Expect

A hands‑on, guided session designed to awaken your creative spirit, foster emotional well‑being, and build practical self‑care skills. Through simple art‑making exercises you'll:

  • Cultivate Mindfulness to increase focus and calm your mind
  • Discover Coping Tools to build resilience
  • Express & Reflect to grow as individuals
     
  • Build Community to share and learn
     

Why Attend?

  • Affordable & Inclusive: Just ₹100—open to everyone.
  • Limited Seats: Only 20 spots for an intimate, focused experience.
  • Lasting Impact: Leave with practical self‑care techniques and a keepsake art piece that embodies your growth.
  • Lead by Fatema Qutbuddin: MFA in Art Therapy with over a decade of experience in guiding creative wellness programs across diverse communities
