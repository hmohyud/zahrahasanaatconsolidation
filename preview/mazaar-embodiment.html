Mazaar-e-Qutbi

An Embodiment of Knowledge and Light

"Their intention is to extinguish Allah’s Light (by blowing) with their mouths: But Allah will complete (the revelation of) His Light, even though the Unbelievers may detest (it)."

Qur'an Majeed

In the context of the creation of the Heavens and the Earth, the Qur’an describes Allah Ta’ala’s throne resting on water. The above ayat conveys the fundamental truth that Allah’s throne – His Dawat is resting on the water of eternal knowledge. Each representative of Allah Ta’ala establishes his Dawat on the foundation of eternal knowledge. What has remained constant throughout the years is that each Imam and each Dai in the Imam’s seclusion, has ascended the throne of Dawat. A throne not made of rubies and gold, or wealth and power but a throne of divine knowledge and wisdom - a throne on water.

Raudat-un-Noor, rests on a flowing body of water and shines ever-bright for all to perceive its divine light. Syedna Qutbuddin’s Dawat continues in his successor in whom the faithful perceive the very same divine light and knowledge. Raudat-un-Noor will forever remain a refuge for those seeking guidance and succour and the star of Syedna Fakhruddin’s Dawat will shine for eternity.
