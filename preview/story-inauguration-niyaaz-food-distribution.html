Inauguration - Niyaaz Food Distribution

October 2018

← All Stories

إِنَّمَا نُطْعِمُكُمْ لِوَجْهِ اللَّهِ لَا نُرِيدُ مِنكُمْ جَزَاءً وَلَا شُكُورًا

"We provide food for you for the sake of Allah alone: no reward do we desire from you, nor thanks.

(Surat al-Insaan: 9)

On 16th Moharram 1439H (6th October 2017),  Syedna Taher Fakhruddin TUS inaugurated the Mazaar-e-Qutbi Niyaaz Jaman in the saya of Roza of Syedna Khuzaima Qutbuddin Saheb RA, inviting people of all faiths and creeds for Niyaaz with the niyat that everyone receives the barakaat of Syedna Qutbuddin RA. It is always in the memory of all Mumineen that whenever they would come for blessings to Syedna Qutbuddin to his home he would invite them “Jaman Jami ne jaajo” for food before they left his hazrat. Today his successor Syedna Fakhruddin Saheb invites all who come to his Roza for Niyaaz.

Everyday hot wholesome vegetarian lunch of dal, rice and a sweet dish is served from 12pm to 2pm. Daily more than 70kgs of rice, 44 kgs of dal and 8 kgs of a sweet dish are prepared. Everyday more than 150 people from all religions and economic backgrounds including industrial workers, construction laborers and children partake in the Niyaaz jaman. There are two full-time staff who manage the operations of the Niyaaz. Mumineen participate in the khidmat of niyaaz and also contribute regularly to join in the sawaab of Mazaar-e-Qutbi Niyaaz.

From October 2017 till October 2018, nearly 50,000 have eaten in Mazaar-e-Qutbi.

May Allah give Syedna Qutbuddin RA the highest position in Janaat-e-firdosh and long life till Qiyamaat to his successor Syedna Fakhruddin TUS.
